A new way of understanding sleep

Our research shows that children struggle with going to bed because they see it as an end to their daily activities. Why sleep if there are so many toys to play with and so much tv to watch?

Turn bedtime into playtime

Our solution is to transform the way sleep is understood by children. Preparing for sleep is boring because sleep is boring. What if going to bed meant reaching another dimension?

Building onto children's imagination

We want to empower children to believe that going to sleep means having the space and tools to be whoever they want to be. By giving them tasks and scenarios, we help them imagine that preparing for sleep actually means getting ready for a space mission that will take you to Mars..
